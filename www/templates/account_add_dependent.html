Eligibility Requirements for Spouse/Dependents

For a Spouse/Dependent to be added, their last name must match yours.

If the last name does not match, or the relationship is a domestic partnership, common law marriage, etc., approved documentation is needed to confirm eligibility. Examples of such documentation include: Marriage license, Domestic Partner Registration/Certificate (varies by state, but needs to be for a one year period), proof of insurance (from employer), mortgage/lease/utility bill (to establish longevity of common law relationship) (state must be a common-law state), and birth certificates. In order for claims to be processed, documentation to verify the relationship may be required.

Approved documentation is needed to confirm eligibility of a dependent child or children. Examples of such documentation include a birth certificate, driver license and/or proof of student status.

Eligibility must be confirmed prior to dependent(s) utilizing benefits under the plan. To submit this documentation, please email info@uslegalservices.net or fax to (904) 730-0023.
